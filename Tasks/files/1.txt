Over 543,000 customers without power
Over 543,000 customers were without power as of 2:15 a.m. ET on Tuesday, according to PowerOutage.us, with Tennessee, Mississippi, Louisiana and Texas hit the hardest.

At least 182,335 customers were without power in Tennessee, 140,577 customers in Mississippi, 100,118 customers in Louisiana and 30,564 in Texas, PowerOutage.us said.

The Nashville Electric Service -- which as of Monday was the hardest-hit utility in the country according to PowerOutage.us -- said it planned to "double its workforce" to respond to the outages.
